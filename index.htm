Isaac's Sock Store


Vetements Socks - Vetements is a French clothing and footwear collective founded by Demna Gvasalia in 2009. Gvasalia reflects a very "pragmatic" approach to fashion.




Gosha Rubchinskiy Socks - Гоша Рубчинский/Gosha Rubchinskiy is an up-and-coming Russian designer. takes inspiration from the fall of the Iron Curtain, Russian street and youth culture, and his experiences to create his collections.




OFF-WHITE Socks - OFF-WHITE by Virgil Abloh sticks true to its classic striped black bars. These socks are very minimalist and are very similar to many of Abloh's designs.
